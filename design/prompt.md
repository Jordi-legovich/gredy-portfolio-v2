Please find below a list of requirements for the look and feel.

## Onepage Design

- The website is a onepage design.
- The first background image covers the entire screen when opened.
- The title on the first section must be visualized at the center of the page
- The fonts are Poppins for text and Monserrat for titles
- On the bottom right there needs to be a "back to top" arrow button: make sure it doesn't overlap with other text
- In the second section Make the text white and the background color like the one found on the footer (instead of the white one)

### Navbar
- Drop the "Home" button and the Instagram and Facebook icons
- It's position is fixed at the top and it stays in position when scrolling
- The background is transparent
- The Logo, "Chis siamo", "Servizi" and "Contatti" buttons are linked to a section of the home page:
	- Logo -> The first section (exactly like when the site is opened)
	- "Chi Siamo" -> The second section (with the white background)
	- "Servizi" -> The third section (the one with the "Club" title)
	- The "Contatti" button leads to the bottom of the page where the "Get in touch" title is located.

## Desktop version
The desktop version has to follow the rules above and the mockup provided.
Every section covers the entire screen
## Mobile Version
- The navbar goes on the bottom of the screen and remains transparent
- The "Chi Siamo" and "Servizi" buttons disappear -> the only buttons visible from mobile are the logo and the "Contatti" buttons
- The back to top button disappears
